# Maintainers

The current maintainer of Kilolock and their scope of authority.
Governance is intentionally minimal at the v0 stage: a single
benevolent-dictator maintainer makes final decisions. This will be
revisited as the project grows additional active contributors.

## Maintainer responsibilities

The maintainer is responsible for:

- Reviewing and merging Pull Requests.
- Cutting releases and tagging versions.
- Maintaining the [roadmap](./README.md#roadmap) and accepting,
  deferring, or declining proposed work.
- Enforcing the [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
- Responding to security reports (see
  [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md#security-issues)).
- Custodianship of the project's trademark
  ([TRADEMARK.md](./TRADEMARK.md)) and CLA archive
  ([cla/icla.md](./cla/icla.md)).

## Decision-making

For technical decisions, the maintainer takes informal community
input and decides. Major architectural decisions are recorded as
ADRs in [`docs/adr/`](./docs/adr/) before they are implemented.

For licensing, trademark, and governance decisions, the maintainer
decides, subject to the constraints of [ADR 0003](./docs/adr/0003-governance-and-monetization.md):

- The OSS license commitment (Apache 2.0) cannot be revoked.
- A future commercial entity may be formed and assigned project
  stewardship, but the OSS commitment survives the assignment.

## Becoming a maintainer

When a second maintainer is added, this document and the associated
governance ADR will be updated to reflect a more formal model
(decision quorum, areas of responsibility, etc.). Until then,
sustained high-quality contributions and engagement with project
direction are the natural path to maintainership; reach out to the
current maintainer to discuss.
